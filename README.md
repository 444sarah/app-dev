# Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

**Frieren: Beyond Journey's End** or also known as **Frieren** is an animated series talking abouts an almost immortal elf, just going about her life, and interacting with people. It follows her realization that while she's an elf who will live for thousands of years, everyone she cares about isn't.

## What makes it so good?

- Despite it being a fantasy anime, it talks about what happened *after* a main lore.
- It doesn't center itself being a romance anime.
- It is relaxing to watch. The animation is amazing.
